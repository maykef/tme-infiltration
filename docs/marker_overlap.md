## Interpretation

Overlap is **LOW** (11.5% Jaccard). Jackson is a breast-epithelial/tumor panel (keratins, ER/PR/HER2, GATA3, E-cadherin, EpCAM-adjacent), Moldoveanu is a melanoma immune panel (CD8a, CD4, FoxP3, PD-L1, LAG3, TIM3, checkpoints, SOX10/S100). Only lineage-agnostic markers (SMA, CD45, CD68, CD20, Ki67, CD3, H3) are shared. This low semantic overlap is a plausible, sufficient explanation for the negative cross-panel transfer observed in Stage 5 — the self-supervised representation learned on breast markers has little corresponding signal in the melanoma panel. Any claim of 'pretraining helps/hurts' must be read in this light.
